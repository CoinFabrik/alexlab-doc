# 💧 DAMM (Discrete Automated Market Maker)

DAMM is a new liquidity model on ALEX that allows you to provide liquidity in **targeted price ranges** called *ticks*. This gives you more control over your position and can lead to higher capital efficiency.

By placing liquidity in a specific tick, you earn a share of the trading fees generated **while the price remains in that range**.
